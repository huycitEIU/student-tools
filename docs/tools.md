# Tools Reference

## Study & Planning

### Study Timer
Focus and break timer with a visible top-bar status.

### My Timetable
Schedule management for classes and recurring events.

### Student Planner
Task planning and study organization.

### Notes
Simple notes workspace for drafting and organizing content.

## Academics

### Course Calculator
Estimate course grades from assignments and weighting.

### Grade Scale
Quick reference for grade conversions.

### Dictionary
Lookup word definitions and related language data.

### Chinese Vocabulary
Store Chinese words with pinyin, type, Sino-Vietnamese reading, and Vietnamese meaning, then search entries quickly.

### English Vocabulary
Store English words with phonetic notes, type, and Vietnamese meaning, then search entries quickly.

### Constants
Browse common math, physics, and chemistry constants in a table and add your own custom constants.

### Practice
Practice flashcards from Chinese and English vocabulary data with card flip, type filtering, and shuffle controls.

### IELTS Writing
Exam-style writing simulation with shared community topics, plain writing area (no writing-assist features), live word count, and stopwatch.

## Utilities

### Weather
Search locations, view forecasts, and save places for later.

### Unit Converter
Convert between common unit types.

### Random
Generate random numbers or pick items from a list.

### QR Generator
Create a QR code from text or a URL, preview it, copy the source content, and download the image.

## Finance

### Money Management
Track budget, income, and expenses.
